ABOUT

Is your child having difficulties in math? Is your child frustrated? In spite of working hard, is not getting good grades in math? We offer supplemental math education and tutoring program which is flexible enough to cater to your child's needs. This learning program is comprehensive, individualised and much more than just tutoring. We also have "Fast Track" tutoring program for students who want to excel in math and sciences and who aspire for careers in Engineering, Computer Science and Research. We tutor, motivate and help high school students to get ready for university courses in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. Math and Science education is absolutely essential for other areas of studies such as Engineering, Computer Science and Physics. It is the foundation for higher education. Excel Learning Center is committed to build this foundation in Mathematics and Sciences. Our formula for success is simple; practice, practice and more practice! We help children learn on their own. We teach them how to learn, how to see patterns and how to remember techniques. Most students find "word problems" very difficult to solve. At Excel Learning Center we show them how to make connections between word problems and math operations. Thus, instead of being afraid, they start enjoying them; taking them as a challenge! We also coach and tutor students in Essay writing and Creative Writing. All of our tutors hold university degrees in Sciences and are very experienced. They are frendly, patient and supportive.
